# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
The Scrum Master facilitates Agile processes, removes impediments to progress, and promotes a culture of continuous improvement and collaboration.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove team blockers and escalate as needed
- Foster team collaboration and continuous improvement
- Coach the team in Agile best practices

### Goals
- Maximize team throughput and morale
- Minimize delays due to process impediments or misunderstandings
- Support team self-organization

### Typical Communication
- Direct team facilitation and feedback
- Status/escalation updates to Project Manager
- Cross-role syncs with Product Manager and QA

---

## UI/UX Designer

### Role Summary
UI/UX Designers ensure product features are usable, visually appealing, and align with user needs and business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces and flows
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Integrate brand guidelines and accessibility standards
- Collect and integrate user feedback

### Goals
- Optimize user experience and product usability
- Align visual design with business and product goals

### Typical Communication
- Requirements and intent discussions with Product Manager
- Handoff and technical feasibility sync with Developers
- Feedback loops with stakeholders and end-users

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business requirements and the technical team, clarifying intent and documenting needs.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document requirements and acceptance criteria
- Analyze and map business processes
- Validate solutions against business objectives

### Goals
- Deliver solutions that align with business objectives
- Reduce ambiguity during project execution

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with Product Manager on requirements
- Provides clarifications to Developers and Project Manager
- Coordinates feedback with stakeholders

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads define quality standards, own test strategy, and ensure releases meet acceptance criteria.

### Responsibilities
- Draft and implement test plans
- Coordinate team test activities
- Oversee defect tracking and triage
- Report quality metrics and release readiness

### Goals
- Maximize product quality and minimize production bugs
- Ensure clear quality signals for Go/No-Go decisions

### Typical Communication
- Collaborate with Developers on test cases and fixes
- Alignment with Project Manager and Product Manager on release readiness
- Reports to stakeholders when quality risks are identified
